Application Tips
Start Early

Most universities have deadlines in Dec–Jan. Begin your applications at least 6 months ahead.

Get Multiple LORs

Aim for 2–3 recommendation letters from professors who know your academic work well.

Statement of Purpose

Write a clear, specific SOP that connects your experience to the program you're applying to.

Standardized Tests

GRE/GMAT for USA, IELTS/TOEFL for English proficiency. Check each university's requirements.
